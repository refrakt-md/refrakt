# v0.18.0 — Contract hardening & deferred polish

Follows v0.17.0's declarative metadata-and-layout model. Two threads: harden
the rune output contract before third-party themes build against it, and clear
the polish items deferred out of v0.17.0.

## Goals

- Remove the legacy `slots` + `structure` shim now that every first-party rune
  is on `metaFields` + `blocks` + `layout` ({% ref "WORK-313" /%}) — the one
  breaking change held back from v0.17.0, with its own changeset.
- Ship the html-scaffold client-behaviors bundle so a scaffolded html site has
  working interactive runes ({% ref "WORK-293" /%}).
- Decompose `plan-progress` into sugar over `aggregate` ({% ref "WORK-296" /%}).
- Begin the rune-contract-hardening specs as they move from draft to scoped:
  declarative structure assembly (`SPEC-081`) and the typed node data channel
  (`SPEC-082`).
